Meet the Team

At Hot Beans Web, we believe that a strong, collaborative team is the key to innovation. Our developers, designers, and strategists work together to build high-quality, modern web solutions. Each member of our team brings unique expertise, creativity, and passion to their role, ensuring that we deliver cutting-edge websites and applications that meet the highest industry standards. With a mix of experienced professionals and rising talent, we foster an environment of continuous learning and development. From crafting intuitive user experiences to writing clean and scalable code, our team thrives on solving complex challenges and pushing the boundaries of web development. We are more than just colleagues—we are a community of tech enthusiasts who share a commitment to excellence, innovation, and teamwork.

Alex Carter – Senior Web Developer

"Coding is like brewing coffee – precision, creativity, and a little bit of magic." Alex has over 8 years of experience in web development, specializing in JavaScript frameworks, backend development, and cloud solutions. He leads our development team and ensures that every project meets industry standards. His expertise in React, Node.js, and database management allows him to develop highly scalable and efficient web applications. Outside of work, Alex enjoys contributing to open-source projects and mentoring aspiring developers in the industry

Sarah Bennett – UI/UX Designer

"A great user experience is invisible, yet unforgettable." Sarah is a creative UI/UX designer with a keen eye for detail. She focuses on crafting user-friendly, aesthetically pleasing, and accessible web interfaces that enhance user engagement. With over 6 years of experience, Sarah has worked on everything from corporate websites to e-commerce platforms, ensuring that every design is both functional and visually appealing. She specializes in Figma, Adobe XD, and front-end styling to create intuitive designs that align with user expectations.

Daniel Reed – Project Manager

"A successful project is built on strong communication and teamwork." Daniel ensures that all our web projects run smoothly. With 5+ years of experience in project management, he keeps the team on track, meets deadlines, and maintains client satisfaction. His expertise in Agile methodologies, sprint planning, and team coordination allows him to oversee multiple projects efficiently. Daniel is passionate about bridging the gap between developers and clients, ensuring that technical aspects are communicated clearly. His leadership fosters a collaborative work environment, helping the team thrive under tight deadlines.
